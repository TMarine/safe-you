WHAT IS SAFE YOU?

Safe YOU is a multi-stakeholder mobile application and platform that is designed to empower and protect women and girls from violence, as well as raise awareness on key gender-based issues.

Users of the dynamic mobile application not only have access to emergency assistance when they need it, they also have access to free forum discussions with service professionals, safe spaces for open peer to peer dialogue, and up to date information about where to seek help.
